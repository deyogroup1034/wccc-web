Summer is one of the hardest seasons for the families we serve. With kids home from school — and the free meals that come with it — many households feel the squeeze. Our pantry shelves empty faster than ever, and we need your help to keep them full.

## Most-needed items

- Peanut butter and jelly
- Canned proteins (tuna, chicken, beans)
- Boxed meals and pasta
- Breakfast cereal and oatmeal
- Canned fruits and vegetables
- Diapers and basic toiletries

> Every bag of groceries is a family that doesn't have to choose between food and rent.

## Three easy ways to help

1. **Donate items** — drop off non-perishables during our open hours. See the details on our [Get Help](/get-help) page.
2. **Host a drive** — rally your church, business, or neighborhood. We'll help you plan it; start on the [Get Involved](/get-involved) page.
3. **Give** — a financial gift lets us buy exactly what's running low. Here's [how to give](/donate).

Thank you for helping our neighbors through the summer. Your generosity keeps hope on the shelves.
