We're excited to welcome you to the brand-new Wylie Christian Care Center website. Our goal was simple: make it easier for our neighbors to find what they need, and easier for our supporters to lend a hand.

## What you'll find here

- **[Get Help](/get-help)** — the programs we offer, who qualifies, what to bring, our hours, and the communities we serve.
- **[Get Involved](/get-involved)** — ways to volunteer, partner your church or business, or organize a food drive.
- **[Donate](/donate)** — how to give today, with secure online giving coming soon.
- **[Contact](/contact)** — reach us by phone, email, or a quick message.

## Built for the people we serve

Whether you're a family looking for help during a hard season or a neighbor wanting to give back, we hope this site makes that next step a little easier.

If you spot something that could be clearer, [let us know](/contact) — we'd love your feedback.

Thank you for being part of this community. Together, we keep bridging the gap and restoring hope.
